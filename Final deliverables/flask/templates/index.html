NOVEL HANDWRITTEN DIGIT RECOGNITION

The handwritten digit recognition is the ability of computers to recognize human handwritten digits. It is a hard task for the machine because handwritten digits are not perfect and can be made with many different flavors. There are a number of ways and algorithms to recognize handwritten digits, including Deep Learning/CNN, SVM, Gaussian Naive Bayes, KNN, Decision Trees, Random Forests, etc. MNIST is a dataset consisting of 70,000 black-and-white images of handwritten digits.
